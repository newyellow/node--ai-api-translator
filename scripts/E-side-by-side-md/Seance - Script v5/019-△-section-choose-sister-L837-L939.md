\+ 互動  
玩家撿起扇子。玩家將扇子放置在通靈板中央。扇子與通靈板各自發出微弱光芒，並各自伸出一道光線。玩家將扇子與通靈板的光線連接，完成連結。

\+ Interaction  
The Player picks up the fan. The Player places the fan in the center of the talking board. Both the fan and the board emit a faint glow, each extending a strand of light. The Player connects the strands from the fan to the talking board, completing the link.

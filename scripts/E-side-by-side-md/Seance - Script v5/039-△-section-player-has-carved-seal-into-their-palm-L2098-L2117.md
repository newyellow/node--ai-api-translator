# △ Section: 玩家已將封印刻在手掌上

# △ Section: Player has carved seal into their palm

\+ 互動

\+ Interaction

如果玩家在手掌上刻有封印儀式，並且在林淑美被惡魔附身時觸碰她，將觸發好結局。

If the player has the sealing ritual carved on their palm, and they touch Lin Shu-Mei while she is possessed by the Demon, this will trigger the Good Ending.

\+ 聲音捕捉（動作）

\+ VolCap (Action)

**（客戶端）** 林淑美坐在椅子上，對你尖叫，雙手抓握。

**(Client)** Lin Shu-Mei is in the chair, screaming at you, grasping with her hands.

\+ 聲音捕捉（對話）

\+ VolCap (Dialog)

情緒：（憤怒）

Emotion: (Furious)

**（客戶端）** 把我的心還給我！

**(Client)** Give me my heart!

_\+ 說明：_

_\+ Explanation:_

_如果玩家未及時觸碰林淑美，將觸發章節：壞結局。_

_If the player does not touch Lin Shu-Mei in time, this triggers Section: Bad Ending._

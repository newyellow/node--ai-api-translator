# △ Section: A final wish

\+ Explanation  
_After the heart is absorbed, the pattern in the Player's palm flashes brilliantly before fading away.  
_

\+ VolCap (Dialog)

**(Brother)** Once you return to the real world, you will need to carve this symbol into your palm. Then imprint it in blood onto Shu-Mei’s skin. This will exorcise the demon and leave it powerless. But! The demon must be inside Shu-Mei when you touch her, understand?

\+ VolCap (Dialog)

**(Adopted Son)** Surely if the demon sees the Medium performing a ritual, it will kill our sister immediately!  

\+ VolCap (Dialog)

**(Brother)** My brother is right. There is only one way. One of us must go with you and fight the demon. To give you time to perform the ritual.  

\+ VolCap (Dialog)

**(Adopted Son)** Take me. I have nothing.  

\+ VolCap (Dialog)

**(Brother)** No. I won’t allow it. Ah-Hui has suffered enough. Let me save my family.  

\+ Voiceover

**(Ghost Choir)** Choose / The camera / Take one

\+ Explanation

_If the player does not do anything, play the following line to push them along:_

\+ VolCap (Dialog)

**(Brother)** One of us must go with you.

The Player uses the camera to capture either the ghosts of Lin Hui OR Lin Wen-zong.  

\+ Explanation  
_If you choose Lin Hui:  
_

\+ VolCap (Dialog)

**(Brother)** He will fight with all he has. Chun-Hua will have every reason to be proud of him. Now: touch me, and I will send you back.  

\+ Explanation  
_If you choose Lin Wen-zong:  
_

\+ VolCap (Dialog)

**(Adopted Son)** He was the best of us in life. He will be so, again, in this. Now: touch me, and I will send you back.

\+ Voiceover

**(Ghost Choir)** Hurry / No time

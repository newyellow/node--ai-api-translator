# △ Section: The hospital

\+ Location

**(Spirit Realm)** The asylum. Lin Chun-hua moves with stiff, uncoordinated jerks, like a person lost to madness, floating aimlessly back and forth.  

\+ Explanation  
_On the walls of the room are three paintings, by Ah-Hui, of Lin Chun-hua in different poses and locations: a garden; a street cafe; beside a lake. On each is an energy seal. As the player points their camera at each one, it will begin to speak: but when the seal is collected with the camera, the dialogue changes. (This is the same mechanic as the father’s scene.) The three dialogues are as follows:  
_

\+ Voiceover

+Emotion (Happy)(Self-Hatred)

**(Lin Chun-Hua)** My wedding will be perfect… I ruined everything…

\+ Voiceover

+Emotion (Happy) (Self-Hatred)

**(Lin Chun-Hua)** I’m so excited to be married… Ah-Hui could never love someone like me!  

\+ Voiceover

+Emotion (Happy) (Self-Hatred) (Desperate)

**(Lin Chun-Hua)** My father will come to accept Ah-Hui… I murdered him… but I didn’t do it!  

\+ Interaction  
The Player finds a thick rope made of tied rags on the floor. The Player picks up the rope.

\+ Voiceover

**(Ghost Choir)** Broken hearted / Broken mind / Lost

\+ VolCap (Action)

**(Sister)** Lin Chun-hua turns and smiles at you, full of the hope and excitement of her wedding day. She rushes towards you; then stops and clutches her head as if fighting off a migraine or a repressed memory.

\+ VolCap (Dialog)

**(Sister)** I don’t want to kill myself! I want to live! I don’t want to die! Ah-Hui is still waiting for me!

\+ Explanation  
_A massive demonic claw descends from the ceiling.  
_

\+ VolCap (Action)

**(Sister)** Under the demon's manipulation, Lin Chun-hua struggles violently as if being strangled.

\+ Explanation  
_The lights cut out completely. The sound of a chair falling echoes through the room. When the lights return, the rope hangs from the ceiling._

\+ Interaction  
The player fights with Lin Chun-Hua. The player defeats her.

\+ Explanation

_Lin Chun-hua lies on the floor in an unnatural position, her eyes wide and staring. Ah-Hui is now in the room._

\+ VolCap (Action)

**(Adopted Son, Sister)** He kneels down beside her body, reaching out to touch her.

\+ VolCap (Dialog)

**(Adopted Son)** Chun-hua? My bride. Why did you do it?

\+ VolCap (Action)

**(Adopted Son, Sister)** We see a significant dark energy transfer from Lin Chun-hua to Ah-Hui. Then he stands, suddenly cold and unemotional.

\+ VolCap (Action)

\+ Emotion (As the demon, menacing)

**(Adopted Son)** (Laughs)

\+ Explanation  
_He dissolves away from sight._

\+ Interaction  
The Player takes one last photo of Lin Chun-Hua.

\+ Voiceover

**(Ghost Choir)** Capture her / Bring her  

\+ VolCap (Action)

**(Sister)** Upon a successful capture, Lin Chun-hua’s voice whispers from inside the camera:  

\+ VolCap (Dialog)

**(Sister)** I didn’t want to… I wanted to live… It made me do it…

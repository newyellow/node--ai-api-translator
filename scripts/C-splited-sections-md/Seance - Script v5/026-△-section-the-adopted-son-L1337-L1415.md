# △ Section: The adopted son

\+ VolCap (Action)

**(Client)** After leaving the portrait, Lin Shu-mei returns to her seat at the desk. She is commanding.  

\+ VolCap (Dialog)

**(Client)** Summon Lin Hui next. The painter. Do it now.  

\+ Interaction  
The Player places the portrait in the center of the table. Both the portrait and the table emit a faint glow, each extending a strand of light. The Player connects the strands from the portrait to the talking board, completing the link.  

\+ Voiceover

**(Ghost Choir)** Lin Hui / The painter / The lost lover...  

\+ Explanation  
_Near the Player’s position, the sound of a brush stroking against a canvas can be heard. The strokes grow increasingly rapid and frantic, accompanied by Lin Hui’s irritable, hate-filled low growls.  
_

\+ VolCap (Dialog)

**(Client)** He’s near, all right. I can smell the rage.  
  
\+ Explanation  
_The sound of a brush violently tearing through canvas rings out. Viscous black paint, looking like blood, oozes from the top of the wall, pooling into the silhouette of a human figure.  
_

\+ VolCap (Dialog)

**(Client)** Is that you, Lin Hui?  

\+ Voiceover

**(Ghost Choir)** Angry / Furious / Sealed inside  

\+ Interaction  
The Player uses automatic writing. Guided by the spiritual sensation, they draw a symbol: Fractured Heart.  

\+ VolCap (Dialog)

**(Client)** I don’t care about your broken heart, Ah-Hui.  

\+ Interaction  
The Player continues automatic writing, tracing out a new shape: Fire.  

\+ VolCap (Dialog)

\+ Emotion (Nasty)

**(Client)** Be as angry as you like, you can’t hurt me!

\+ Interaction  
The Player continues automatic writing, tracing out a new shape: Trading House Logo.

\+ VolCap (Dialog)

\+ Emotion (Nasty)

**(Client)** What even is that? Enough of this. Send him back to the pit he came from.  

\+ Voiceover

**(Ghost Choir)** The bell / send him back

\+ Interaction  
The Player rings the silver bell, and the surroundings return to a state of calm.
